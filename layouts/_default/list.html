About Me

Thanks for checking out my portfolio. I am a wife, mom, and career transitioner. I make a living as a website developer and application designer. My technical speciality is in the realm of UI design and engineering. I love making then taking an idea from Figma to the digital screen with HTML, CSS, and Javascript. I have fun creating youtube videos for my dance and coding channels respectively, as well as participating in game jams and meetups.

Yes, I am one of those unicorns who can Code and Design. All of the projects in this portfolio are original designs and coding endeavors. Don't be fooled, I have years of experience working on small and medium sized teams. Teamwork makes the dream work.

I graduated from art school (where I learned web development) and went on to teach movement and wellness classes in New York and New Jersey. In 2019 I decided to settle down in Georgia and get serious about freelancing as a generalist designer, developer, and writer. I realized my passion was working with startups and small businesses as a team member that would wear many hats while helping the brand polish and refine their voice through behind the scenes work and direct interactions with customers and clients.
